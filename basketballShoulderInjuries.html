Most Common Basketball Shoulder Injuries


Torn Rotator Cuff

Summary: a tear in the tissues connecting muscle to bone around the shoulder joint often caused by repetitio of the same movement

Symptoms: shoulder pain and weakness

Treatment: rest, medication, physical therapy, corticosteroid injections, and surgery



Dislocated Shoulder

Summary: the humerous comes out of the shoulder blade socket

Symptoms: out of place shoulder, swelling, pain, and lack of flexibility in shoulder

Treatment: moving the bone back in place, splinting, medication, and rehabilitation



Shoulder Lacerations

Summary: wound that occurs when the skin, tissue, and/or muscle is cut open

Symptoms: bleeding and burning feeling in area of laceration

Treatment: stitches



SLAP Tear

Summary: SLAP, or Superior Labrum Anterior and Posterior, is an injury to the labrum of the shoulder caused by acute trauma or repetitive movement

Symptoms: locking/popping/grinding of the shoulder, pain when lifting objects, and decrease in shoulder strength

Treatment: medication, physical therapy, and arthroscopy
