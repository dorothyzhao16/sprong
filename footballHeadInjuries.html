Most Common Football Head Injuries


Concussion

Summary: shaking of the brain inside of the skull usually caused by a blow to the head or torso

Symptoms: headache, confusion, memory loss, nausea, dizziness, and fatigue

Treatment: pain medicatons and activity restrictions



Cerebral Contusion
Summary: bruise of the brain tissue often caused by impact to the skull

Symptoms: headache, confusion, fatigue, nausea, and seizures

Treatment: prevention of hypotesion (low blood pressure), hypercapnia (CO2 in blood), and hyponatremia (lack of sodium); however surgery may be necessary



Subdural Hematoma
Summary: collection of blood outside the brain caused by severe head injury

Symptoms: loss of consciousness, change of behavior, and weakness

Treatment: burr hole trephiation, craniotomy, craniectomy



Skull Fracture
Summary: break in the cranial caused primarily by an impact to the head

Symptoms: swelling, facial bruising, and beeding from nostrils or ears

Treatment: pain medication or surgery in severe cases
