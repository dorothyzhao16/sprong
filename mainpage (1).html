Sprong: When Sports Go Wrong

Welcome to Sprong!

A site where we provide advice for athletes of all levels in any kind of medical emergency with easy-to-use software and tips from others who have suffered similar injuries.


First, pick the sport that you play.

Once you have chosen your sport, pick your injury type: bone or muscle.

Next, select the region where your injury is on the body.

You will find a sports injury diagnosis and treatment option(s) once you select the correct area of the body.
